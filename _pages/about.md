My name is Muhammet Özkaraca. I am currently working as a project assistant at the International Centre for Migration Policy Development (ICMPD). Previously, I received my **master's degree in Political Science at the Central European University, Vienna/Austria** and completed my **B.A. in International Relations at the Bilkent University, Ankara/Turkey**.

My academic research interests are broadly focused on political violence and armed conflicts, with an emphasis on dynamics of non-state armed groups. I am also interested in border security and migration management. Methodologically, I use mixed-methods research designs.
